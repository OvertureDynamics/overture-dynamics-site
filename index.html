The Game

Two pillars. One living world.

Project Oath is built on two inseparable systems — real-time action combat that demands genuine skill, and an AI-governed world simulation that evolves without developer scripting. Neither exists without the other.

Pillar I  ·  World Simulation

A World That Lives

Five specialized AI agents — Economy, Weather, Ecology, Geology, and Narrative — run the world continuously. No developer scripts the content after launch. The world generates its own history, lore, and crises in real time.

  • Economy driven by real supply and demand — prices emerge from player behavior
  • Ecological over-harvesting depletes biomes permanently until recovery
  • Geological events reshape geography — the world accumulates a living record
  • AI Narrative Engine generates emergent lore NPCs reference in conversation

Pillar II  ·  Combat System

Combat Without Limits

No class selection at creation. Your equipped weapon defines your identity, abilities, and role. Swap weapons, swap playstyle. Six core archetypes at launch — advanced techniques are discovered by the community, not scripted into tutorials.

  • Greatsword · Dual Blades · Staff · Bow · War Hammer · Grimoire
  • Physics-respecting hitboxes — no tab-targeting. Positional play matters
  • Modular signature weapon crafting — your build, permanently yours
  • One weapon system for PvP arenas, open-world conflict, and raids

ERC-8004  ·  NPC Standard

NPCs That Remember You

Every NPC in Project Oath runs as an ERC-8004 agent — a persistent, on-chain entity with genuine memory of player interactions, faction loyalty, and autonomous ownership of in-world assets. Citizens are remembered. Wanderers are strangers.

  • Citizen NPCs remember you permanently across every session
  • Relationships, history, and reputation persist and compound over time
  • Your offline player agent remains active in the world as a Citizen
  • World historian system logs major events — players live through history
Overture Dynamics

An AI-first studio building the world's most immersive online world.

Founded in New York in 2025, Overture Dynamics was built on a single conviction: the MMO genre has stagnated because its worlds are scripted. Every dungeon, every NPC, every weather event — authored by a developer, replayed in a loop. Players don't inhabit a world. They perform inside one.

We're building a world governed entirely by AI agents. The economy finds its own equilibrium. Biomes deplete and recover. Geological events reshape geography. NPCs build genuine relationships with players across years of play — because they actually remember.

Project Oath is our flagship title. It is also our proof-of-concept for the ERC-8004 standard — the foundational architecture for truly autonomous, on-chain NPC entities. We believe that standard will outlast any single game. The world doesn't need scripting when it runs itself.
