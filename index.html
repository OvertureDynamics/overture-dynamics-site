Development Roadmap

The path to a living world.

A 9-year, 5-phase build — each phase unlocking the next layer of the world simulation.

2026 – 2027  ·  Phase 1

Foundation · Pre-Seed & Seed

Raising $500K pre-seed to build the three proof-of-concept assets — ERC-8004 NPC prototype, UE5 combat vertical slice, and GDD. Seed round of $3M closes by end of 2027 after live NPC demo, playable combat build, and 1,000+ Discord members. First full-time engineering and AI hires join. Economy Agent goes live.

$500K Pre-Seed ERC-8004 Prototype Combat Vertical Slice $3M Seed Close Founder's Pass Launch Economy Agent Live

2028  ·  Phase 2

Pre-Alpha · Series A

$15M Series A raises on demonstrated NPC stability and all simulation agents integrated. A 10–18 person team builds toward internal alpha. Weapon crafting, marketplace v1, and all five world agents run simultaneously for the first time.

$15M Series A All 5 Agents Integrated Internal Alpha Marketplace v1

2029 – 2030  ·  Phase 3

Closed Alpha

Founder's Pass holders access the world — the only way to play during this phase. Full world simulation runs live for the first time with real players. 1K-player closed alpha. Series B preparation begins.

Founders-Only Access Full World Simulation Live Series B Prep 1K-Player Alpha

2031 – 2032  ·  Phase 4

Open Beta · Full Subscription Launch

Public subscription launch. 5K–20K players, full subscription launch, economy tuning, press & influencer push. $500K MRR target by end of 2032 — $6M subscription ARR.

Open Beta $500K MRR Target 5K–20K Players Series B / C

2033 – 2034  ·  Phase 5

Release · The Living World

Full public launch targeting 200,000–500,000 registered players. ~120K subscribers generating $21.6M subscription ARR plus marketplace fees. On-chain ownership layer activates. The world runs itself — entirely.

Public Launch 200K–500K Players $20M–$25M ARR On-Chain Ownership Activates
